Blini (Russian pancakes)

Description:

Blini are small bite-sized pancakes, usually made from buckwheat, but all-purpose flour works just as well, and usually topped with a creme-fraiche, or even smoked salmon. A super easy, and tasty way to start your day!

Ingredients required:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour.
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder.
  • 3/4 cup milk.
  • 2 tablespoons milk.
    • <1i>1 large egg.
  • 1 tablespoon melted unsalted butter.
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter.

Steps to make blini:

    First combine flour, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
    Then whisk 3/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons milk, egg, and 1 tablespoon milted butter together in a seperate bowl. Mix into the flour mixture until the batter is fully combined.
    Heat 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Cooking several blini at a time, drop batter, one tablespoon at a time, onto the heated skillet. Cook until bubbles form, 1 1/2 to minutes. Flip and continue cooking until brown, about 1 minute more. Move to a paper towel-lined plate to help soak up excess butter.
    Repeat this process as much as desired with remaining batter.
