Hoosier Cream Pie

Description:

Hoosier cream pie is a sweet custar delicacy made with simple ingredients, and is considered a desperation pie as it's made without eggs. It's a common place desert dish found in Indiana, the land of the Hoosiers.

Ingredients required:

  • 1 cup whipping cream.
  • 1 cup half and half coffee creamer.
  • 3/4 teaspoon vanilla.
  • 1 cup sugar.
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt.
  • 1 tablespoon butter.
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg.
  • Pastry lined 9-inch pie pan.

Steps to make some Hoosier cream pie:

  1. Combine half and half coffee cream, whipping cream, and vanilla.
  2. in another bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. Slowly, using an electric mixer or a wire whisk, add cream mixture to the flour mixture until smooth.
  4. Let stand while rolling out the pastry crust.
  5. Line a 9-inch pie pan with pastry; dotting bottom with small pieces of butter.
  6. Spinkle nutmeg evenly over the bottom of the pie shell.
  7. Briefly beat the cream mixture again before pouring into the prepared pie shell.
  8. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes.
  9. stir the filling and lower heat to 325 degrees.
  10. Bake 20-25 minutes longer.
  11. Shake the pie pan every 8-10 minutes while baking until done.

Serves 8, and enjoy a little slice of Indiana!
